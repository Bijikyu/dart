---+ EvolSayer

A simulator for RNA structure evolution using the [[TKF Structure Tree]] model.
Part of the EvolDeeds package.

	* How to get it: DownloadingDart
	* Browse CVS: DartPerl:evolsayer.pl
	* Example tree files in DartSrcDir:evoldoer/t
